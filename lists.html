The hype list

I'm not hyped about a lot of things. but when I am. I am REALLY hyped. So I compiled this list so I dont forget about these when they finally release.

Ori and the will of the wisps

The new ori game has upgraded visuals, upgraded music and upgraded gameplay. It's everything I could of hoped for and more. The latest teaser trailer showed a whopping 5 boss battles and amazing new abilities for Ori. This is gonna be an amazing game for sure.

11 february 2020

Ori the game

The stanley parable: Ultra Deluxe

The stanley parable was an indie game hit back in 2014. The narration and great humor is what made it so good. The funny way of story telling was good enough to come back to it every year even though I've memorized the entire script by now. The Ultra Deluxe version has more story and more endings. releases on consoles.

2019

Stanley parable

Hollow knight: silksong

Hollow knight was a kickstarter project that went really well but only started to gain players a year after release. It went really well when they released on switch. Team cherry has released 3 free DLC and promised another one. but instead of making a new DLC. They made it an entire new game and I couldn't be more happy.

2019

Hollow knight: silksong

Celeste chapter 9

Celeste is a great game with an amazing amount of detail and character. It's brutally hard but never punishing. Its a lot of content for it's price but people wanted more. Celeste chapter 7 has over 100 new levels. new music and great level design. It releases quite soon as well.

ETA july 2019

Celeste game

Links awakening

New installments in the Legend of Zelda franchise always make me super happy. This time its a highly stylized version of an older zelda game. From the trailers it seems to have a dungeon level editor included. It's very exciting, I trust it's going to be great fun.

20 september 2019

Store page

The legend of zelda: Breath of the Wild Sequel

Breath of the wild was in the making for 5 years and developed a new engine for the game specifically. It would be mad to not use the engine again. I've been aching for more Breath of the Wild content and a sequel is perfect. No release date yet, hopefully not another 6 years.

In the next 20 years

Announcement video

Deltarune: chapter 2

Deltarune is an anagram of Undertale. So it's basically a sequel to undertale. The first chapter was for free and still had the amazing charm that undertale had. New characters, new story, new music, new gameplay. I can't wait for more chapters in the Deltarune series. No release date yet tho.
